# Outreach Messages

## Co-working space / casual

Ever argued with someone about whether a color is teal or green? Turquoise or aqua? I built a Chrome extension called **Color Namer** that's basically a giant experiment in exactly that.

Every time you open a new tab, you get a color and one question: what would you call it? No right answers — just what comes to mind. The goal is to see how different people name the same colors.

Install it here: https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/color-namer/pkmmbffclkcmdcnbcpbebekphlnnfdba

Takes 10 seconds to install and you'll see it the next time you open a tab. Would love to hear what you think!
